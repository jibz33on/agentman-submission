Apple Inc. Q1 2025 Earnings Call — Sample Transcript (abbreviated for demo)

Tim Cook (CEO):
Good afternoon everyone. We're pleased to report record revenue of $124.3 billion for the December quarter, up 4% year over year. iPhone revenue came in at $69.1 billion. Services hit an all-time high of $26.3 billion, up 14% year over year. We now have over 1 billion paid subscriptions across our platform.

We're seeing strong momentum with Apple Intelligence. The features we've rolled out are driving real engagement and we believe this is still very early days. We're expanding Apple Intelligence to more languages and regions in April.

Luca Maestri (CFO):
Gross margin was 46.9%, up from 45.9% a year ago. We returned over $30 billion to shareholders during the quarter through dividends and buybacks. Our installed base of active devices reached an all-time high across all product categories and all geographic segments.

For the March quarter, we expect revenue to grow in the low to mid single digits year over year. We expect gross margin between 46.5% and 47.5%.

Analyst Q&A:

Analyst (Morgan Stanley): How are you thinking about AI monetization longer term?
Tim Cook: We're focused on making the experience great first. We believe Apple Intelligence will be a driver of upgrade cycles and services growth. We're not ready to put a specific number on it yet but we're very bullish.

Analyst (Goldman Sachs): Can you talk about China performance?
Tim Cook: Greater China was down 11% year over year, which was below our expectations. We're working hard on the business there. We believe the market remains important and long-term opportunity is intact.
